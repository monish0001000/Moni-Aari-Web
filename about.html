About Moni-Aari

Preserving tradition, crafting excellence

Our Story

Founded by MONISHA in 2000, Moni-Aari has been at the forefront of preserving and celebrating the ancient art of Aari embroidery. What began as a passion for traditional craftsmanship has blossomed into a trusted name in custom embroidery services.

For over two decades, we have dedicated ourselves to mastering the intricate techniques of Aari work, passed down through generations. Each stitch tells a story, each design carries the weight of tradition, and each piece we create is a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Our Mission

At Moni-Aari, our mission is to blend the timeless beauty of traditional Aari embroidery with contemporary design sensibilities. We believe in creating pieces that not only honor our cultural heritage but also resonate with modern aesthetics.

We strive to make handcrafted embroidery accessible to everyone, from brides seeking the perfect bridal blouse to fashion enthusiasts looking to add a unique touch to their wardrobe. Every project we undertake is approached with the same dedication to quality and attention to detail.
